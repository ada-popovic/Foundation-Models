I. Sudowrite

I woke up very early in the morning. As always, the only thing I could hear was the wildly uninteresting sounds of the forest. It was the same damned forest, day after day. It was starting to kill me. I had been on the run since I was a teenager and the only thing that had changed was the scenery.

I waited for an hour to make sure that the forest was still empty, then I finally got up and got ready to go fishing. The phone call with Alexei had woken me up. I had been planning to make a move right then, but he had other plans. “I have to wait until my friends have found you a safe house. It is the only thing that is keeping me alive.” He had said. I knew that I couldn’t trust him, but the thought of taking care of a baby was overwhelming. The baby and the mother. How did I end up with this in my life? I had been hiding from the world for years and now I couldn’t seem to walk away.
